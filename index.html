BeautyGuard AI - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

BeautyGuard AI is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application. By using BeautyGuard AI, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. No account required. We do not collect name, email, phone, or location data. We do not track your behavior across other apps or websites.

3. Photos and Camera Usage

Photos are temporarily sent to our AI service for ingredient analysis only. NOT stored on servers after analysis. NOT used to train AI models. NOT shared with third parties beyond the AI analysis provider.

4. Data Stored on Your Device

Scan history is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device. You can delete it anytime from Settings. Uninstalling the app removes all data permanently.

5. AI Service Provider

We use a third-party AI vision service. Images are sent securely over HTTPS. No personal information is shared — only the image you choose to scan is transmitted.

6. Children's Privacy

BeautyGuard AI is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Data Security

All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS protocols. Scan data is stored only on your local device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

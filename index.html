2. Information We Do NOT Collect
BeautyGuard AI is designed with privacy at its core. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII). We do not require you to create an account. 3. Photos and Camera Usage
When you scan a beauty or cosmetic product, the photo you take is temporarily sent to our AI service solely for ingredient analysis. The photo is NOT stored on our servers after analysis is complete. The photo is NOT used to train AI models. The photo is NOT shared with any third parties other than the AI service provider. All photos are processed in real-time and immediately discarded after the result is returned to your device.
4. Data Stored on Your Device
Scan history (product name, safety score, ingredient counts, and scan date) is stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device. You can delete your scan history at any time from the Settings screen. Uninstalling the app permanently removes all locally stored data.
5. AI Service Provider
BeautyGuard AI uses a third-party AI vision service to analyze product ingredient photos. The images are sent securely over HTTPS. We do not share any personal information with the AI provider — only the image you choose to scan is transmitted.
6. Children's Privacy
BeautyGuard AI is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used this app, please contact us immediately.
7. Data Security
All data transmission between the app and AI services is encrypted using industry-standard HTTPS/TLS protocols. Scan data is stored only on your local device and is not accessible to us or any third party.
